# TransitOps

Smart Transport Operations Platform

## Team
Hackathon Team

## Problem Statement
A web platform to manage vehicles, drivers, trips, maintenance, fuel expenses and analytics for logistics companies.

## Planned Features

- Login with Role Based Access
- Dashboard
- Vehicle Management
- Driver Management
- Trip Management
- Maintenance Tracking
- Fuel & Expense Tracking
- Reports & Analytics

Backend:
